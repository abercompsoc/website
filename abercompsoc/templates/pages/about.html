Welcome to the new website for the Aberystwyth Computer Science Society, or AberCompSoc

We are a group of students at Aberystwyth with a keen interest in all things Comp Sci. The society aims to provide an environment where like minded people can meet and discuss topics of interest. We also aim to attend various events and work on projects for fun.

We have frequent socials every other week. They usually start at 8PM at the Cambrian Hotel

We also host events around the year, such as trips to Bletchley Park, museums, and Student Show and Tell Events. We also host an annual trip to FOSDEM, which takes place in Brussels every year around the start of February.

We are Sponsored by the BCS - The Chartered Institute for IT. As a BCS Student Chapter, we are able to supplement your academic experience with our links to the BCS and member groups, as well as provide activities and events.

The best way to keep in touch is to join our group on Facebook. We will post any upcoming events or news here.
